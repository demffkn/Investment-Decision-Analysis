# Customer Base Analysis: KazMarket Online Retail

### Project Description
[cite_start]This project involves a comprehensive analysis of the customer base for KazMarket, an online retail company[cite: 2]. [cite_start]Using a dataset of approximately 31,800 customers, the study focuses on customer segmentation, purchasing behavior inequality, and lifecycle trends following the initial transaction[cite: 3, 5].

### Key Insights from Analysis
* [cite_start]**Decile Analysis:** The study revealed significant inequality in value creation, with the top 10% of customers generating approximately 39-40% of the company's total revenue and profit[cite: 6].
* [cite_start]**Cohort Analysis (2021):** Analysis of the 2021 customer group shows a typical trend where customer activity and the number of active users gradually decline in subsequent quarters after the first purchase[cite: 8, 9, 10].
* [cite_start]**Cohort Comparison:** A three-cohort analysis demonstrated that earlier cohorts contribute higher cumulative revenue and profit due to their longer engagement history with the platform[cite: 11, 12].
* [cite_start]**Strategic Outlook:** While the customer base is considered moderately healthy, the high dependency on a small percentage of top-tier customers presents a potential risk[cite: 14, 16].
